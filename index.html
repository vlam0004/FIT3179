World Population Information 2020

The following page contains maps and graphs that represent global population data

General Population Map/h2>

This map shows the the general global population estimates of 2020 for majority of the countries in each continent. Hovering over each country provides you with the country details as well as its population.

Population Density Map

This map shows the population density of countries where that is calculated based off the number of people and the amount of land if the population was evenly distributed across all the space of the country. It is shown on the map that the Asia region is one of the most highly dense areas in the world, with large amount of land area and population, whereas majority of the globe is not as densely populated. This suggests that majority of the global population arises from Asia.
